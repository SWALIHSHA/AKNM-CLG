About College

AKNM Government Polytechnic College was established in 1994 at Chelari, in Malappuram district. It is one of the well reputed AICTE approved institutes affiliated to the Directorate of Technical Education, Kerala.
AKNM Government Polytechnic institute offers three diploma courses across Engineering stream. Apart from this the institute also offers two part time diploma courses in Electronics & Communication and Computer Engineering branches. Admissions to all courses are based on the Centralised Admission Process. AKNM Govt. Polytechnic College, Thirurangadi, established in 1994, is one of the well reputed AICTE, New Delhi approved technical institution in Malappuram District, under Government sector affiliated to the Directorate of Technical Education, Kerala state. The college is situated at Chelari, near to national highway 66 in Thirurangadi Taluk. The Campus spreads over 6.23 acres of land and offers Diploma courses in Electronics, Electronics & Communication and Computer Engineering branches. More than 465 students are studying in these different branches and about 60 staff are also working in the institution in a serene atmosphere.
